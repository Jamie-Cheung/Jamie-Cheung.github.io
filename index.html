I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang University in Hangzhou. Before that, I received my M.S. in Computer Science and Technology from Jiangnan University, Wuxi (2019.09 - 2022.06), and my B.E. in Internet of Things Engineering from Jiangsu University, Zhenjiang (2015.09 - 2019.06).

My research interests include AIGC, 3D Generation/Reconstruction, Embodied Intelligence, and Medical Image Analysis.

From 2024.11 to 2025.03, I worked as a Research Scientist Intern at 360, Beijing, supervised by Ao Ma. From 2025.04, I am a Research Scientist Intern at vivo, supervised by Zhendong Zhang. Working with them has been a pleasant experience, and I am excited about jointly moving toward AGI. I welcome students and collaborators who are interested in exploring AGI together. If you are interested in internship opportunities with Ao Ma and Zhendong Zhang, I am also happy to help introduce you.

Please feel free to email me via zhangzhanj@126.com or cszzj@zju.edu.cn.
